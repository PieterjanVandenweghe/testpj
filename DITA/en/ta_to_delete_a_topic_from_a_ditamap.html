To delete a topic from a DITA map

  1. In the DITA Maps Manager, select the topic you wish to remove from the DITA map.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • Right-click the topic and choose Delete.
    • Press the Delete key on your keyboard.
    Note: When you delete topics in this manner, you only delete their link in the DITA map structure; the files themselves still remain on your computer and can be added again later. Similarly, if you delete topics from your computer, don't forget to also delete them from your DITA map.
