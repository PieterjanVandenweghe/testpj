To add navigation titles to the topics in a DITA map

Navigation titles are used when the actual title of the topic is not appropriate for use in a table of contents, navigation pane, or online content. For instance, when the actual title is too long. Navigation titles will not replace the title within the topic itself or in the topic's generated output.
  1. Choose one of the following:
    • If you are making a new topic reference, click Insert Topic Reference  at the top of the DITA Maps Manager.
    • If you want to add a navigation title to a topic already in your DITA map, right-click your topic in the DITA Maps Manager and select Edit Properties.
  2. At the top of the dialog window (Insert Reference or Edit Topic Reference Properties depending on your choice earlier), enter the navigation title in the space at the bottom right of the window.
  3. (optional) To make this navigation title appear as the title for your topic in the DITA map structure, select Lock.
    Note: You can also change the navigation title by changing the topic reference's attributes. You do this by opening your DITA map in the Editor area and selecting the topicref. You enter a new title in the @navtitle attribute and lock it by setting the @locktitle attribute to yes.
