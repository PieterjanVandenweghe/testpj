Exporting DITA Maps

By exporting your DITA map, you copy your map with all of its referenced information (including images) into a (zipped) folder of your choosing.

Exporting your DITA Map can be useful to make complete back-ups or zipped files to send to people you work with.

  1. Open your DITA map in your DITA Maps Manager.
  2. Select DITA Maps > Export DITA Map
  3. In the Export DITA Map dialog window, select the destination of your exported file.
  4. Select Export as ZIP archive if you want your file to be zipped into a compact folder.
    Note: If you choose to zip your file, you can also enter a new name for the zipped file.
  5. Click OK.
    The exported file is generated in the location you specified.
