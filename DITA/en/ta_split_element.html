To split an element

  1. Place your cursor in the element that you want to split, on the position where you want to split it.
    For example, to split one paragraph into two paragraphs place your cursor inside the <p> element on the position where you want to end the first paragraph.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • Right-click and choose Refactoring > Split element.
    • Press Enter, select Split <current element> and press Enter again.
