Guidelines for topic-based authoring

Well-written, topic-based documentation yields major benefits for users: they find the information they need much faster, can accomplish their goals more efficiently and only have to read the information they are interested in. Writing good topics, however, takes some practice. This topic includes some guidelines to get you started.
