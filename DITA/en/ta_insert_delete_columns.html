To insert and delete columns

  1. Place your mouse pointer above the column that you want to delete or the column to the right of which you want to insert a column.
    Your mouse pointer turns into an arrow and the column is highlighted in blue.
  2. Right-click and select Table>Insert ColumnorDelete Column.
